Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our tools and services

What is EveryTool.tech?

EveryTool.tech is a comprehensive platform offering a wide range of online tools designed to make your digital tasks easier. From text manipulation to file conversion, we provide free, easy-to-use tools that help you work more efficiently.

Are the tools free to use?

Yes, all our tools are completely free to use. We support our platform through non-intrusive advertisements, allowing us to provide these tools at no cost to our users.

How do I use the tools?

Using our tools is simple:

  • Browse to the tool you need from our tools page
  • Follow the on-screen instructions
  • Input your data or upload your files
  • Get your results instantly
Is my data safe?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All processing is done in your browser when possible, and we don't store your data on our servers. For more details, please read our Privacy Policy.

How can I report an issue?

If you encounter any issues or have suggestions, you can:

  • Use our contact form
  • Report issues on our GitHub repository
  • Email us directly at support@everytool.tech

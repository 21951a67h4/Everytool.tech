Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about contacting us.

How quickly will I receive a response?

I typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please include "URGENT" in your subject line.

What information should I include in my message?

To help us assist you better, please include:

  • Your name and contact information
  • Clear description of your question or issue
  • Any relevant screenshots or error messages
  • Browser and device information if reporting a technical issue

Can I report a bug or suggest a feature?

Yes! We welcome all feedback. When reporting a bug, please include:

  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Expected vs actual behavior
  • Screenshots or screen recordings if possible
  • Your browser and device information

For feature suggestions, please explain how it would benefit users. You can also check our Tools page to see what's already available.

Do you offer technical support?

Yes, we provide technical support for all our tools. Our support team can help with:

  • Tool usage questions
  • Technical issues
  • Browser compatibility problems
  • General troubleshooting

For specific tool issues, please visit the tool's page on our Tools page for detailed documentation.

How can I stay updated about new features?

You can stay updated by:

  • Following us on social media
  • Subscribing to our newsletter
  • Checking our Tools page for new additions
  • Visiting our website regularly
